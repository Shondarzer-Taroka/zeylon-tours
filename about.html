Our Story

Zeylona Tours was born from a simple dream – to share the breathtaking beauty and rich culture of Sri Lanka with travelers from around the world. Founded in 2018 by local Ella residents, our journey began with a single tuk-tuk and an enormous passion for our homeland.

Growing up in the misty hills of Ella, surrounded by tea plantations and cascading waterfalls, we witnessed firsthand the magic that our island nation holds. Every sunrise over Little Adam's Peak, every cup of Ceylon tea shared with friends, every story passed down through generations – these moments shaped our vision.

Today, Zeylona Tours has grown into a trusted name in authentic Sri Lankan tourism, but our heart remains the same. We believe that the best way to experience a place is through the people who live there, love there, and call it home.

Our Mission

To create unforgettable, authentic experiences that connect travelers with the true spirit of Sri Lanka while supporting local communities and preserving our natural heritage.
